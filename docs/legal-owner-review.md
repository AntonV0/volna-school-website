# Legal Owner Review Checklist

Public-safe checklist for final owner/legal review of the privacy and refund
pages. This file is not legal advice and should not contain private contracts,
invoices, payment records, customer messages, staff details, or reviewer-only
comments.

## Current Public Page Status

- `/privacy-policy` and `/ru/privacy-policy` are draft privacy notices.
- `/refund-policy` and `/ru/refund-policy` are draft refund policies.
- The pages intentionally show an owner/legal review notice.
- The pages intentionally use `noindex, follow` metadata and are omitted from
  the sitemap while they are drafts.
- Effective dates remain "Owner review required" until the owner approves final
  text.
- Russian text should be checked as legal wording, not treated as automatically
  approved because the English draft is approved.

## Privacy Review Decisions

Owner/legal reviewer should confirm:

- who the data controller or responsible business owner is
- the correct privacy contact details
- what data is collected through enquiries, trial registrations, lessons,
  invoices, private portals, and direct communications
- the lawful basis for each major processing activity
- how children's information and parent/guardian contact details are handled
- whether any special category, safeguarding, or sensitive information is
  collected outside the first website form
- actual service provider categories or names that should appear in the policy
- whether data leaves the UK or EEA and how that should be described
- retention periods for enquiries, trial leads, enrolled learner records,
  invoices, payment records, teaching notes, and admin logs
- cookie, analytics, Turnstile, and consent wording
- how visitors can access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or receive a
  copy of their data
- how consent can be changed or withdrawn after the first choice

## Refund Review Decisions

Owner/legal reviewer should confirm:

- the exact payment model for group courses, private tuition, trials, and any
  future deposits
- when fees are due and how invoices are issued
- whether notice periods differ for group classes and private tuition
- whether same-day absence, no-show, illness, teacher cancellation, or technical
  problems are refunded, credited, rescheduled, or charged
- whether paid course blocks, subscriptions, or prepaid lesson packages have
  different cancellation terms
- refund method, timing, and who approves refunds
- wording for statutory rights that cannot be excluded
- whether remote or online lesson sales need additional consumer cancellation
  wording
- how disputes, chargebacks, or payment reconciliation issues are handled
- whether the Russian policy should be a direct translation or separately
  reviewed policy wording

## Public Repo Hygiene

Safe to record:

- "approved", "changes requested", or "pending"
- public route names
- broad decisions without private names or amounts
- the date a page was approved, if the owner wants that public

Do not record:

- copied private terms from contracts or invoices
- customer examples or submitted form data
- private reviewer comments
- unpublished staff or client information
- payment references, invoice numbers, or account details

## Launch Gate

Before removing the review notices or publishing legal pages as final, confirm:

- English privacy page approved
- Russian privacy page approved
- English refund page approved
- Russian refund page approved
- effective date approved
- retention decisions approved
- consent and cookie decisions approved
- final text checked against the actual production systems and providers
- `noindex` metadata removed if the pages should be searchable
- sitemap inclusion restored if the pages should be searchable
